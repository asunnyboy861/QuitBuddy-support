Getting Started

Welcome to QuitBuddy! Here's how to get the most out of the app:

  • Set Your Quit Date: When you first open the app, you'll be guided through a quick setup to record your smoking habits and quit date.
  • Track Cravings: Log cravings as they happen. Tap "Log a Craving" on the Home screen to record intensity, triggers, and notes.
  • Find a Partner: Go to Settings > Find a Partner to share your partner code and stay accountable together.
  • View Progress: Your dashboard shows time smoke-free, money saved, and cigarettes avoided in real time.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How is money saved calculated? We use the cigarettes-per-day and price-per-pack you entered during setup to estimate your daily savings.
  • Can I change my quit date? Currently, you can reset your data in Settings to start fresh with a new quit date.
  • Is my data shared? Your data stays on your device. Craving logs shared with your partner only go through the partner connection.
  • How does the partner system work? Share your unique partner code with a friend. When they enter your code, you'll be connected for mutual accountability.
